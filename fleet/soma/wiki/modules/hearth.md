# Hearth

> Kitchen and grocery management via Grocy.

## What it does

Hearth connects to Grocy to manage pantry inventory, shopping lists, and meal planning. Lumina can suggest recipes based on available ingredients and track what needs restocking.

## Deploys to

CT310 (YOUR_FLEET_SERVER_IP) at `/opt/lumina-fleet/hearth/`

See `terminus/hearth_tools.py` for available tools.
