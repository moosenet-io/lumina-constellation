# Odyssey

> Travel planning and credit card points optimizer.

## What it does

Odyssey tracks travel destinations, credit card rewards, and loyalty programs. Lumina can recommend which card to use for a purchase to maximize points and track upcoming travel plans.

## Deploys to

CT310 (YOUR_FLEET_SERVER_IP) at `/opt/lumina-fleet/odyssey/`

See `terminus/odyssey_tools.py` for available tools.
